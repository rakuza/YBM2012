Changing the colour of your shape

How to change the Colour of your Shape


1. Select your shape by double clicking on it


2. Click on the box on the Tool bar

3. Select your color of choice, if none of the color suit continue to step 4

4. If none of the colors suited then click on advanced highlighted in green

5.Move the dot and the sliders till you find a colour your happy with and then click off into another part of the screen
